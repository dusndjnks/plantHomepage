MONSTERA


  • Monstera plants are native to the tropical regions of Central and
    South America, where they grow as epiphytes in the dense rainforests.
  • The distinctive holes and splits in the leaves of the Monstera plant,
    known as fenestrations, serve to increase leaf surface area, allowing
    the plant to capture more sunlight and thrive in its natural habitat.
